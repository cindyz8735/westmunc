Who can attend WestMUNC? How do I sign up?


Any high school student is welcome to attend WestMUNC. No Model United Nations experience is necessary, just an interest in learning about Model UN and willingness to participate. To register, click here. Registration closes on Friday, Sept. 16, 2016 at 11:59 p.m.

How much does WestMUNC cost? How do I pay the delegate fee?


All delegates are asked to pay a $5 delegate fee, either cash or check made payable to Cindy Zhang. If you go to school in the San Mateo Union High School District, please give your money to your club leader before the conference. Otherwise, bring your fee on the day of. If the fee is a hardship to you, please email Cindy Zhang.

What do I need to bring to WestMUNC?


Bring any research that you have already done (print it out beforehand) and a printed copy of your position paper, if you wrote one. Please bring something to write with as well and some paper to write on.

Can I use laptops or electronic devices during committee?


You may use a laptop or phone to work on resolutions or research during unmoderated caucuses only. Phones, laptops and other electronic devices will not be allowed at other times during committee. However, you are free to use your device(s) during lunch and the break. Note that there will not be any printer access so please print anything that you need prior to the conference.

Will there be food provided? Should I bring a lunch?


We will be providing pizza and limited snacks. However, we recommend bringing some of your own snacks and we highly recommend bringing water. Please note that we are unable to accommodate dietary restrictions, so please plan accordingly.

Do I need to write a position paper? When is it due?


While position papers are not required, they are highly recommended. Position papers need to be emailed to your chairs by Monday, Sept. 17 at 11:59 p.m. For tips and guidelines on writing a position paper, click here.

How do I write a position paper? What guidelines should I follow?


The United Nations Association of the USA has a good guide on writing a position paper here. For WestMUNC, please keep papers to a maximum of 3 pages, with additional room for citations. Always make sure you cite your sources! If you have more specific questions about your position paper, please email your chairs.

Will there be awards at WestMUNC? How do I win one?


In each of the four committees (WWI: Allies, WWI: Central Powers, EU and UNHRC), there will be a best delegate award, an outstanding delegate award and one or more verbal commendation awards. You do not need to have experience to win these awards. They are based on a variety of factors, including your public speaking skills, ability to work with others and your work in the committee (including position papers and any resolutions that you pass).

What if I have another question?


For committee-specific questions, please email your chairs. If you do not know who your chairs are, please refer to the committees page. For any other question, please email Cindy Zhang.
